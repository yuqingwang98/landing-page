Meet my Guinea pigs

Frodo is the drama queen!

He always squeaks loudly to attract attention.

Recently trying hard to chew his way out.

Buzz is always chill.

He barely fights back when he is being held. Deep down, he might just be very shocked.

Sam is a chubby boy now.

He enjoys most of his day napping and running away from his brother Frodo.

Woody is the smartest.

He is driven by food and would easily forgive anyone if treats are offered as apologies.

Food recommendation

Guinea pigs need fresh vegetables everyday for Vitamin C.
Here are some of their favourites.

  • Bell Peppers
  • Cos Lettuce
  • Coriander
